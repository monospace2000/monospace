My Drumset

  
Interactive drumkit diagram    

Many drummers like to draw detailed diagrams of their drumkit setup. I decided to make mine interactive.
Artwork: Hens Breet

Note: link will open in a new window.
   
Move the cursor over a drum or cymbal, and when the description popup appears, click to hear it sound.

   
The cursor changes to match the drum or cymbal it is over.
