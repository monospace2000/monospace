Today's musings:

On Wednesday July 11, Dutch rock phenomenon Herman Brood jumped to his death from the roof of the Amsterdam Hilton hotel, leaving his fans and the nation in shock. Herman Brood, who was from my home town of Zwolle, was arguably Holland's wildest rocker and the nation's best known (and loved) drug addict. What a way to go Herman! We will miss you forever.
