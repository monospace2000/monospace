About monospace

      monospace is a typical home page: A collection of links, writings and other projects by its owner. It is not a "blog", 'cause I don't need no stinkin' formula in order to have a home page. Just kidding. It's kind of like a blog, but cooler, because I wrote all the tools for it myself, long before the blog craze.

      What it most certainly is not, is a diary. I don't feel the need to spill my inner thoughts all over a public Web page. If you want to know how I feel, just buy me a couple of beers. Instead, this is the place where I experiment with all the latest and greatest toys and tools to create Web content. Technologies used, and misused, in order to bring you this compelling experience: HTML, Dynamic HTML, JavaScript, Cascading Stylesheets, Shockwave, Flash, QuickTime, Server Side Includes, Perl, PHP—and lots of coffee, beer, and cigarettes.

      monospace also has a habit of changing into something else when no one is watching...

      People often inquire whether this domain name is for sale. Unless you're offering an amount upwards of $1,000,000 (that's right, a cool million), don't even bother asking OK?


    About the owner:

      monospace is owned by me, Hendrik Waander Breet, better known as Hens. That's my picture on the left. I'm an artist living in New York City.
