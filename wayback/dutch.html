Dutch word of the day

The Dutch word of the day is, well, a Dutch word, carefully selected by me each day, from memory. That's because I am Dutch, and Dutch is a peculiar language.

Words are chosen not necessarily for their meaning -- in fact most of the words up here I picked because they sound or look interesting.
